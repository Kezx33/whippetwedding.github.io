How do I get to the Venue?

We have no plans to arrange a coach from Dundee, however we hope these directions will help! Oswald House is located just off of Dunnikier way and can be reached by turning right at Kirkcaldy High School.

Is there a Coach?

We have organised a coach leaving from Dundee Science Centre at 6.30pm (please be there by 6.20pm) which will take evening guests to the venue. The coach will then leave Oswald House at 12.30am to take evening and day guests back to Dundee Science Centre.

Is there a Dress Code?

There is no formal dress code but we hope to see you all in your glad rags, gents feel free to wear your best tartan! Any white dresses will risk a red wine spill!

When should I RSVP by?

We would love to see everyone there and we would ask you to RSVP as soon as possible! Whether or not you can can make the celebration please RSVP by 6th July 2024 at the very latest. Anyone who responds after this date will need to bring a chair and a sandwich!

Can I bring my kids?

While we love your little ones, we want you to relax and enjoy the evening. Think of it like a date night! We appreciate you making arrangements ahead of time and leaving the kids at home so you can celebrate with us

Is there a gift list?

Whilst any gifts are appreciated, as most of you know we moved into our lovely home at the end of 2022 and as we built this ourselves we still have some rooms to furnish and projects to finish. We would really appreciate a gift of money to help us complete our dream home!
